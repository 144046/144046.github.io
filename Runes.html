Raw Spells Scalings:

2d10 per level damage or heal for single target spells, halved for temporary hit points.
2d6 per level damage or heal for multiple targets spells, halved for temporary hit points.
1d6 per level for summons or repeated sources of damage or heals for single target spells.
1d4 per level for summons or repeated sources of damage or heals for multiple targets spells.
Some runes will have their own scaling written in italic.

Component Runes. Absence of component rune will make the raw spell need verbal, somatic and material components; each component rune added will remove their component representation from the raw spell's requirements.
  • Orotorem.

    • Spellcasting focus bonus: You don't need verbal components when casting spells.

  • Concentio.
    • Type: Component Rune.
    • Representation: Somatic Component.

  • Corporis.
    • Type: Component Rune.
    • Representation: Material Component.
    • Spellcasting focus bonus: You don't need material components when casting spells. (Unless the spell has components with costs higher than 50GP).

Range Runes. Absence of range rune will make the spell have a range of touch.
  • Sui.
    • Type: Range Rune.
    • Representation: Range of Self.
    • Spellcasting focus bonus: Your spells with a range of self don't need somatic or verbal components.

  • Tactus.
    • Type: Range Rune.
    • Representation: Range of Touch.
    • Spellcasting focus bonus: Your spells with a range of Touch have a 50% chance to affect a second targe within range.

  • Proxime. Raw spells starts from 5 feet, with a 5 feet increment for each raw spell level.
    • Type: Range Rune.
    • Representation: Range from 5 feet to 30 feet.
    • Spellcasting focus bonus: Your spells with a range from 5 feet to 30 feet don't need to see the target to be casted.

  • Iuxta. Raw spells starts from 60 feet, with a 5 feet increment for each raw spell level.
    • Type: Range Rune.
    • Representation: Range from 60 feet to 90 feet.
    • Spellcasting focus bonus: Your spells with a range from 60 feet to 90 feet have their range extended by 50%.

  • Gloria. Raw spells starts from 120 feet, with a 5 feet increment for each raw spell level.
    • Type: Range Rune.
    • Representation: Range from 120 feet to 500 feet.
    • Spellcasting focus bonus: Your spells with a range from 120 feet to 500 feet have their range extended by 50%.

  • Terra.
    • Type: Range Rune.
    • Representation: Range of 1 mile or further.
    • Spellcasting focus bonus: Your spells with a range of 1 mile or further have their range extended by 50%.


Duration Runes. Absence of duration rune will make the raw spell last for 1 turn. All durations higher than instantaneous require concentration.
  • Subita.
    • Type: Duration Rune.
    • Representation: Instantaneous Duration.
    • Spellcasting focus bonus: Your spells with a duration of instantaneous can be cast as a bonus action, this feature can't be used again until you finish a long rest.

  • Dubito. Raw spells starts from 1 round, with a 2 rounds increment for each raw spell level.
    • Type: Duration Rune.
    • Representation: Duration of Under 1 Minute .
    • Spellcasting focus bonus: Your spells with a duration of under 1 minute lasts twice as long.

  • Proelium. Raw spells starts from 1 minute, with a 2 minutes increment for each raw spell level.
    • Type: Duration Rune.
    • Representation: Duration from 1 Minute to 10 Minutes.
    • Spellcasting focus bonus: Your spells with a duration from 1 minute to 10 minutes lasts twice as long.

  • Occasus. Raw spells starts from 1 hour, with a 1 hour increment for each raw spell level.
    • Type: Duration Rune.
    • Representation: Duration from 1 Hour to 8 Hours.
    • Spellcasting focus bonus: Your spells with a duration from 1 hour to 8 hours lasts 50% more.

  • Solis. Raw spells starts from 9 hours, with a 2 hours increment for each raw spell level.
    • Type: Duration Rune.
    • Representation: Duration from 9 Hours to 24 Hours.
    • Spellcasting focus bonus: Your spells with a duration from 9 hours to 24 hours lasts 50% more.

  • Mensis. Raw spells starts from 2 days, with a 4 days increment for each raw spell level.
    • Type: Duration Rune.
    • Representation: Duration from 2 Days to 30 Days.
    • Spellcasting focus bonus: Your spells with a duration from 2 days to 30 days lasts 50% more.

  • Praxis. You can choose some condition under which the raw spell will trigger; once the condition are met, from the point in which you cast the raw spell initially, the desired effect will take place with a duration of 1 turn.
    • Type: Duration Rune.
    • Representation: Until Dispelled or Triggered.
    • Spellcasting focus bonus: Your spells with a duration of until dispelled or triggered can be cast without expending a spell slot, this feature can't be used again until you finish a long rest.

    • Type: Area Rune.
    • Representation: Cone Area.

  • Globus. 5 feet radius sphere or circle for each raw spell level.
    • Type: Area Rune.

    • Representation: Cylinder Area.
    • Spellcasting focus bonus: Your spells with an area of cylinder increase their radius by 5 feet or their height by 10 feet.
